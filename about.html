    About Founder

    Dr. Sharon Mercy, from a humble beginning, being an orphan, a domestic violence survivor later a widow and a single mother, did not let her situations crush her down. She instead rose up, fought back to live and love. Instead of letting her past pull her down, she is determined to make the present and future a better place. With the little she had, she founded an NGO called H.I.V.E Foundation abbreviated as helping the Infected and the Vulnerable Embrace life. HIVE is a complete non-profit, people oriented NGO which solely aims to help the infected, vulnerable, orphan, destitute women to embrace life to the fullest. She is a philanthropist and an active motivational speaker. She fights for the cause of justice. Being founder and director of this NGO she runs community welfare centers at slum area; she provides completely free education to the underprivileged; she trains poor woman with occupational skills, she conducts free awareness programs; provides free health care via medical camps and dental camps etc; she pays hospital visits to comfort patientâ€™s wounds and their souls; she visits many schools to encourage and motivate young generation , for she believes seeding needs to be done at young age for world to become a better place; She even had an orphanage for approx 10 years for orphan children and HIV infected women; which was later shutdown due to scanty resources; She is the voice of minority. People who are down-trodden and underprivileged (slum, fisherman, physically challenged, HIV/AIDS infected, TB infected) come to her for emotional support. She always shares the little she has for herself. But most of all, she shares love All these are executed by a single woman, with literally zero human resource and zero funding. She sold a small piece of land and completely invested it for the needy.A single woman, who was tormented to death, rose up against all odds to help and nurture her fellow destitute to fight their battle; hoping that one day.....one day that she could contribute to making this world a better place and that would add meaning to her life. Rewarded or Not, she is a true leader. Her tigress spirit to combat horrors of society with hope, love and positivity makes her an influential personality on every soil she steps in. Forlornly, Until now Dr. Sharon Mercy has never publicized her great achievement.. She is a hidden and a rare Gem. If brought into the spotlight, her determination and self-less ambition will save millions.
