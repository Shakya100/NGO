    About Our Charity

    Hive foundation is a Non profitable, Non Governmental Organization, aiming to provide care and compassion and extend its support to infected, destitute and vulnerable population. Established in 2005, Hive has been committed to build capacity, livelihood and augment quality enhanced life years to its beneficiaries.

    HIVE has adapted a slum area called Bharathi nagar in Tamil Nadu, India. On November 2016, HIVE collected field data to understand and analyze the needs and the demands of this vulnerable community; furthermore interventions were strategically planned to serve this community. This slum is known for its high prevalence of illiteracy rate, low socio-economic status, poor infrastructure and poor quality of life. On January 2017, HIVE founded a community welfare center at Bharathi nagar.

    Our Mission

    The Infected health and Medical Aspects:
    Fully equipped and functional Hospitals for all diseases.
    Medical college for medical/ dental students, Nurses, Lab technicians and paramedics
    Medical research institute
    Doctors, Staffs and trainees quarters
    Shelter homes HIV/AIDS infected, terminally ill, visually impaired, physically challenged and mentally ill.

    Our Vission

    Social activities among the vulnerable
    Shelter homes for orphans/ semi orphans, underprivileged children, destitute women, old aged homes.
    Schools, colleges, universities and other educational institutions
    Occupational and skill enhancing institution and rural area development centers for Computer education, tailoring and embroidery training etc.
    Self help group and free basic amenities supply for destitute
    Fitness centre, music classes and dance classes
